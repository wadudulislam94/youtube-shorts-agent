Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2025  ·  Anime Shorts Bot

Summary: Anime Shorts Bot does not collect, store, or sell any personal data from viewers or third parties. The App only accesses your own social media accounts using credentials you provide.

1. Who We Are

Anime Shorts Bot is an automated content creation tool that generates AI anime story videos and publishes them to social media platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) using official platform APIs.

2. Data We Collect

The App collects minimal data necessary to operate:

  • API Credentials: TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube API tokens you provide. These are stored only in your private GitHub repository Secrets and your local .env file. They are never transmitted to any third party.
  • Run Logs: Local logs of video titles, publish times, and video IDs. These are stored only on your machine or in your private GitHub Actions artifacts.
  • No viewer data: The App does not collect any data from people who watch your videos.

3. How We Use Data

  • API tokens are used exclusively to publish content to your own social media accounts
  • Run logs are used for debugging and monitoring the pipeline
  • No data is used for advertising, tracking, or any commercial purpose

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any data with third parties. The only data exchange that occurs is between your accounts and the official APIs of:

  • TikTok (via TikTok Content Posting API)
  • Meta / Instagram (via Meta Graph API)
  • Google / YouTube (via YouTube Data API v3)

Each platform's own privacy policy applies to data handled by their APIs.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • API credentials are stored as encrypted GitHub Secrets (if using GitHub Actions) or in a local .env file on your own machine
  • No data is stored on external servers operated by Anime Shorts Bot
  • Temporary video files uploaded to CDN (catbox.moe) for Instagram publishing are automatically deleted after 72 hours

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Please review their privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Meta Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Pollinations AI (image generation — no personal data sent)
  • Catbox.moe (temporary video hosting for Instagram)

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Since the App does not store personal data about users, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns, please contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
